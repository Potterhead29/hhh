Shrimp Pasta.

I could eat this all day everyday!

Ingredients needed

  • Butter
  • Shrimp
  • oil
  • garlic
  • pepper
  • nokoss
  • pasta
  • water
  • salt

Recipe

  1. First season shrimp with nokoss
  2. Pour oil in pan
  3. When oil is hot pour butter
  4. Get about 10 pieces of garlic smash then throw in buttter
  5. Put shrimp in let it sit for 5 minutes
  6. Pour a good a good amout of water close pan and let the shrimp cook
  7. In another pot boil water
  8. When water is boiled put salt then pasta
  9. Drain water put butter in your pasta
  10. When you see the shrimp forming sauce turn stove off
  11. Serve your food.
